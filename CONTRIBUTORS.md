## Credits for the initial dataset

The 115-leader initial dataset was assembled from public sources including:

- Stanford HAI 2025 AI Index Report
- Nobel Prize, Kavli Foundation, MacArthur Foundation, and Breakthrough Prize publications
- Google Scholar and Semantic Scholar citation data
- Institutional faculty pages at universities including Stanford, MIT, UC Berkeley, Harvard, Caltech, UChicago, IIT Bombay, Johns Hopkins, and Cambridge
- Wire service reporting from Reuters, Associated Press, Bloomberg, and the Financial Times
- Company and venture funding announcements verified through SEC filings where available
- Direct researcher biographies on verified institutional and lab websites

This is not an exhaustive source list — each leader's entry in `data/leaders/` carries its own sources. The initial assembly drew on hundreds of individual sources, and this project exists to make those sources transparent and verifiable for every claim.
